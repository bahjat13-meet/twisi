About

TWISI is a free, fun and educational app that aims to teach young children the values of open-mindedness and acceptance of different cultures through drawing.

Through TWISI, we aim to mitigate the ignorance which has fueled the Israeli-Palestinian conflict. Doing so, we hope to shape the next generation for a better future.
